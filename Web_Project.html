Viet Nam History

Dien Bien Phu victory(May 7, 1954) is one of the most prideful events in Viet Nam and the world. The viectory regard Vo Nguyen Giap general as the most well-known general in world. Moreever, Dien Bien Phu is the strongest fortress in 20 century. Vietnamese destroy it with basic weapons. Moreever, they used bike to load rice to the battlefield and the victory is the pride of Vietnamese.

August revolution in Viet Nam(August 14, 1945) is a great event in Viet Nam. The revolution againsted French and the origin of revolution is from Viet Minh movement and it spreaded all over the country. After the success of the revolution, Ho Chi Minh president read the Declaration of Indenpendence of Viet Nam in September 2, 1945

Independence day in Viet Nam(September 2, 1945) is the most prideful day in Viet Nam because in September 2, 1945, Ho Chi Minh president read the Decleration of Independence in Ha Noi after the August Revolution. The Independence day was the day Viet Nam became an Independence nation in the world.
